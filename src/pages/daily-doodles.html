Daily Doodles

In order to make creativity a daily exercise, I start each morning by drawing in my “Year of the Doodle” sketchbook. This sketchbook provides me with daily prompts that I use as inspiration to come up with creative illustrations. I use a variety of mediums to create my illustrations including: pencils, colored pencils, pens, watercolor, and gouache paints.

I create my doodles using pens, pencils, and gouache paints.
