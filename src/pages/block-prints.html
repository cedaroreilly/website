Block Prints

The following are all hand-carved linoleum block prints. To create these prints, I transfered my design onto a piece of linoleum that I then carved away with v-shaped gouges. The raised areas of the design are then inked with a brayer and printed by hand onto Japanese kozo paper.

Linoleum block prints are a variation of traditional woodblock printing.

All my designs begin with original artwork that I transfer onto linoleum blocks.
