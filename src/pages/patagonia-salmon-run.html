Patagonia Salmon Run

Every year, Patagonia puts on a benefit 5k race to raise money for local environmental groups. I created this artwork as the logo and t-shirt design for the 2016 Patagonia Salmon Run, which benefited Once Upon a Watershed, a local organization working to restore Ventura county’s wetlands and watersheds. The artwork I created is a hand-carved linoleum blockprint.

The artwork I created was used on race t-shirts, Patagonia’s website, and other promotional materials.
